    Our Story

    Two years ago we embarked on a mission: to provide Crown Heights with the highest quality and best tasting kosher sushi possible. At the Sushi Spot we believe that sushi making is an art form that combines flavor, texture, aroma and aesthetics to create the perfect dish. With a passion for utilizing only the freshest and finest ingredients we have had the pleasure of serving thousands of happy customers both in our store and at our celebrated events.

    Providing a wholesome environment, quality products, excellent pricing and exceptional customer service is at the heart of the Sushi Spot. We take pride in catering to the needs of our unique and special community.

    Thank you for welcoming us into your home.

    Yossi & Dovid

    Free Delivery

    We believe that delivery should be both fast and free. We pride ourselves on providing the fastest delivery time in Crown Heights. Whether you order in store, on the phone or online we will expedite your order so you have your meal when you want it. All for free!

    Curb-Side Pickup

    When we introduced curb-side pickup is was a revelation. We understand that our customers are busy, so simply phone in or order online and we will deliver your order to your car. Now that is convenience.

    Online Ordering

    Ordering online should be simple, quick and efficient; when building our platform we had that in mind. With our system you can browse our menu, choose your rolls, place your order and have your sushi in a flash. This is online ordering for the 21st Century.
